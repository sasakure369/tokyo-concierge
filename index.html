私たちの想い
WHY WE DO THIS

We believe everyone who comes to Japan deserves more than the tourist version of it.

01

Japan Concierge started behind a cocktail bar in Ginza.

One of our founders, Akira, spent his nights mixing drinks for travelers from all over the world. Night after night, he heard the same thing: "We wanted to go somewhere special — but we couldn't book it."

02

The best spots in Japan often take reservations by phone only, in Japanese. Many places barely show up online in English.

So visitors ended up going to the same handful of famous restaurants as everyone else — and kept asking him one question:

Isn't there somewhere only locals know about? Somewhere we could actually get into? — what travelers asked Akira, night after night
03

We've felt it from the other side too. Traveling abroad, we always wished we had a local friend we could just ask — someone to handle the booking, the language, the little things that stand between you and a perfect night.

So we built exactly that. We're the local friend who picks up the phone for you. Tell us where you want to go — famous or hidden, simple or impossible — and we'll do everything we can to get you in.

Because the memory of one perfect evening in Japan can stay with you for the rest of your life.
We'd love to help you make it.

— Akira & the Japan Concierge team

FAQ
What if the reservation isn't available?
We'll suggest the best alternatives. If none work for you, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
How long does it take to confirm?
We aim to confirm within 24 hours. For popular spots, it may take up to 48 hours depending on the venue.
Can I request any place in Tokyo?
Yes! Restaurants, tourist spots, cultural experiences, and more. Just let us know what you have in mind.
Do I need to speak Japanese?
Not at all. We handle all communication with venues in Japanese on your behalf.
How will I receive my confirmation?
Via DM on Instagram or the SNS handle you provide. Make sure to follow us so we can reach you!
When can I reach you? (response hours)
We reply within 24 hours, every day. Requests sent late at night are handled the next morning (Japan time). For urgent same-day needs, mention it in your message and we'll do our best.
How do refunds work?
If we can't secure your reservation and none of our alternatives work for you, just message us by DM or email (y0useipc@gmail.com). We'll refund the full fee through Stripe, usually within 5–10 business days.
