FAQ

What if the reservation isn't available?
We'll suggest the best alternatives. If none work for you, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
How long does it take to confirm?
We aim to confirm within 24 hours. For popular spots, it may take up to 48 hours depending on the venue's response time.
Can I request any place in Tokyo?
Yes! We handle all kinds of reservations — restaurants, tourist spots, cultural experiences, and more. Just let us know what you have in mind.
Do I need to speak Japanese?
Not at all. That's exactly why we're here. We handle all communication with venues in Japanese on your behalf.
How will I receive my confirmation?
We'll contact you via DM on Instagram or the SNS handle you provide. Make sure to follow us so we can reach you!
