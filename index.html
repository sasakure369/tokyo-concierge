WHY WE DO THIS

We believe everyone who comes to Japan deserves more than the tourist version of it.

01

OMOTENASHI DESK started behind a cocktail bar in Ginza.

One of our founders, Akira, spent his nights mixing drinks for travelers from all over the world. Night after night, he heard the same thing: "We wanted to go somewhere special — but we couldn't book it."

02

The best spots in Japan often take reservations by phone only, in Japanese. Many places barely show up online in English.

So visitors ended up going to the same handful of famous restaurants as everyone else — and kept asking him one question:

Isn't there somewhere only locals know about? Somewhere we could actually get into? — what travelers asked Akira, night after night
03

We've felt it from the other side too. Traveling abroad, we always wished we had a local friend we could just ask — someone to handle the bookings, navigate the language, and take care of all the little things that stand between you and a great trip.

So we built exactly that. We're your local friends in Japan. Tell us where you want to go and what kind of experience you're after — famous or hidden, simple or impossible — and we'll handle everything we can to make it happen.

Because the memory of a perfect trip to Japan — the meal you couldn't have found alone, the place only locals know — can stay with you for the rest of your life.
We'd love to help you make it.

— Akira & the OMOTENASHI DESK team

FAQ
How does the whole process work?
Simple: (1) choose a plan — Standard or Premium — and pay securely via Stripe, (2) fill in your details on the form right after payment, (3) we confirm your booking and reach out by email. Premium members also get ongoing chat support via Instagram DM, WhatsApp, or LINE throughout their stay. Most confirmations arrive within 24 hours.
What's included in Premium, and why is the price variable?
Premium includes everything in Standard, plus a custom itinerary map tailored to your stay, trip-planning support (we work with you over a few days to build your full schedule), niche spot research, and unlimited chat during your trip. The price starts at $60 and varies depending on the number of reservations and the length of your stay — we'll confirm the final price after reviewing your request before any additional charge.
What if the reservation isn't available?
We'll suggest the best alternatives. If none work for you, we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked.
How will you contact me?
Booking confirmations and important updates are always sent by email, for every plan. Real-time chat support over Instagram DM (and WhatsApp / LINE on request) is included with the Premium plan only — this keeps our response quality high for everyone.
Can I request any place in Japan?
Yes! Restaurants, attractions, cultural experiences, tours and more — across Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka and beyond. Just let us know what you have in mind.
Do I need to speak Japanese?
Not at all. We handle all communication with venues in Japanese on your behalf.
What are the booking badges?
Each spot may show a status badge: Book Early (reserve 1+ month ahead), Details Needed (extra info like membership required), Deposit (a deposit is needed to hold the booking), Hard to Book and Very Hard to Book (high demand — we'll do our very best). They help you plan with realistic expectations.
How do deposits work?
Some venues (marked Deposit) require a deposit to secure your booking. After you pay for your plan and send your details, we'll send a separate secure payment link for the deposit. Deposits are generally non-refundable once the booking is placed — see our cancellation policy below.
What is your cancellation policy?
Once a reservation is confirmed, our standard policy is no cancellation. Whether changes or refunds are possible depends entirely on each venue's own rules — some charge in full even for same-day changes. Please plan with plenty of buffer. (If we fail to secure your booking, you're always fully refunded.)
How do refunds work?
If we can't secure your reservation and none of our alternatives work for you, just message us by DM or email (y0useipc@gmail.com). We'll refund the full service fee through Stripe, usually within 5–10 business days.
