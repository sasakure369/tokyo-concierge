How We Work

利用規約
In short: you tell us where you'd like to go, and we make the booking for you. Here are the details, so there are no surprises.

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Japan Concierge ("we", "us", "our"). By using our reservation service, you ("you", "the customer") agree to the terms below. We've tried to keep them friendly and clear.

1. What We Do

We're your local friend in Tokyo. We contact restaurants, attractions, and experience venues on your behalf and request a booking based on your instructions. We act as an intermediary that arranges reservations — we don't own or operate the venues themselves.

2. Our Fee

Our service fee is a flat 2,000 JPY per reservation request. This is the only fee we charge. Any food, drinks, admission, or service charges set by the venue are separate, and you pay those directly to the venue.

3. Making a Request

When you submit a request, please give us accurate details — your name, contact info, the venue, your preferred date, and party size. We can't be responsible for a booking that fails because of incorrect or incomplete information.

4. Bookings Are Not Guaranteed

Availability is always up to the venue. We can't promise that any specific reservation can be secured. But here's our promise: if we can't get you in, we'll suggest great alternatives — and if none work for you, you get a full refund.

5. Your Part

  • Please arrive on time for your reservation.
  • Please follow any cancellation rules, fees, or deposits required by the venue.
  • Please respect the etiquette and rules of each place you visit.

6. Our Responsibility

We can't take responsibility for the food, service, or experience the venue provides, or for any loss or inconvenience during your visit. Our responsibility is limited to our reservation assistance, and at most to a refund of our service fee.

7. Updates

We may update these terms occasionally. The latest version always lives on this page.

Our Refund Promise

返金ポリシー
We want you to book with total peace of mind. So our promise is simple:

Last updated: June 2026

If we can't secure your reservation and you don't want an alternative, you get a 100% full refund of the 2,000 JPY fee. No stress, no hassle.

1. You Get a Full Refund When…

  • We're unable to secure your requested reservation, and
  • You decide not to accept any of the alternatives we propose.

2. Refunds Don't Apply When…

  • The reservation was successfully booked as requested.
  • You cancel or don't show up after the booking was confirmed.
  • The booking failed because of incorrect details provided by you.

3. How to Get Your Refund

Just reach out to us by Instagram DM or email. Refunds are processed through Stripe to your original payment method, usually within 5–10 business days (depending on your card issuer).

4. About Venue Charges

Any deposit or charge you pay directly to a venue follows that venue's own refund and cancellation policy, which is outside our control.

Your Privacy

プライバシーポリシー
Your trust matters to us. Here's exactly what we do — and don't do — with your information.

Last updated: June 2026

1. What We Collect

When you make a request, we collect your name, email, SNS handle, the venue you'd like, your preferred date, party size, and any special requests.

2. How We Use It

We use your information only to arrange your reservation and to keep in touch with you about it. When booking, we share with the venue just the minimum needed — usually your name, date, and party size.

3. Payment

Payments are handled securely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card details.

4. We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We only share what's necessary with the venue to complete your booking, and with trusted providers like Stripe and Google to run our service.

5. Keeping Your Data

We keep your reservation details only as long as needed to provide our service and meet legal or accounting requirements.

6. Questions?

Anytime — just message us on Instagram or email y0useipc@gmail.com.
